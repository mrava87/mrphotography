Matteo Ravasi

Hi there! My name is Matteo Ravasi and I am a research scientist with passion for landscape photography. I have been exposed to photography since a young age and inherited this passion from my father. In 2012 I finally decided to make the big step of buying my first DSLR camera. Since then I have been travelling to Brazil, Iceland, Italy, and China and I currently live in Norway, one of the best countries for outdoor photography.

If you find any of my photos interesing and want to know more about how and where I took them, just drop me a message and I would be glad to help.
